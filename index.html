We always welcome reader submissions. If you're proud of a project you worked on or have a beautiful product to sell and you'd like to see it reach a wide audience, we'd love to hear from you.


Just make sure the submission includes:

  • has high-quality professional photos available
  • has a web link to either the maker or retailer
